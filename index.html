About American International Tutorial

Since 2024, AIT has been at the forefront of providing world-class British education in Bangladesh. We combine the rigor of the Edexcel curriculum with Islamic values and local cultural understanding.

Our mission is to nurture confident, capable, and caring global citizens who are prepared to make meaningful contributions to society while maintaining their Islamic identity and Bangladeshi heritage.

Principal's Message

"We Learn, We Lead, We Inspire"

Principal

"

I am delighted to welcome you to the American International Tutorial. I have a vision to build a nation that is honest, moral, industrious, skilled, and intelligent—one that follows the path of Islam as described in the Holy Qur'an and taught by Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

As a Muslim, I believe that without strong religious beliefs, one cannot lead a truly moral life. Everyone should strive to be a true Muslim. Furthermore, other religions also uphold moral values. Almighty Allah provides wealth equally among Muslims, Hindus, Buddhists, and Christians, so why shouldn't we respect one another? We must show respect to people of all religions.

To fulfill our vision, we will focus on Islamic studies and Qur'an education. We will provide support for Hifz according to parents' preferences and ensure religious studies are available for students of other faiths.

We are following the American and British education systems, and we aim to present all teaching materials in a clear, scientific, and digital format based on their syllabuses. We emphasize fluency in English, mathematics, science, history, geography, Bangladesh studies, and ICT.

We encourage students to learn additional languages, such as German, Spanish, or French, and will organize art and craft activities as well as other extracurricular opportunities.

Finally, entrust us with your child, and we will return to you a moral, honest, skilled, and confident young person.

"
Principal
